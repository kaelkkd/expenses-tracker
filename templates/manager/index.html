Welcome to EPT, your comprehensive web platform for efficient expense management and tracking. EPT, short for Expense Tracker, is built on Django and designed to simplify the process of recording and monitoring your expenditures. With EPT, you can effortlessly log all your financial transactions, providing a centralized hub to manage your expenses seamlessly. What sets EPT apart is its ability to generate detailed monthly summaries, allowing you to gain valuable insights into your spending patterns over time. Visualizing your financial data is made easy with EPT's intuitive table displays. The platform presents your expense information in clear and organized tables directly on the website, making it convenient for you to analyze, understand, and make informed decisions about your budget. Take control of your finances with EPT – the ultimate tool for expense tracking and management. Sign up for free today and embark on a journey towards financial clarity and stability.

Getting started

Getting started with EPT is quick and easy. To begin your journey towards effective expense management, follow these simple steps:


1. Create Your Account: Click on the "Register" button in the header and fill in your basic information, including your email and name. We value your privacy and only ask for essential details to personalize your experience.


2. Login: If you've already created an account, click on the "Login" button in the header. Enter your registered email and password to access your EPT dashboard.


Once logged in, you'll have full access to the powerful features of EPT. Start recording your transactions, generating monthly summaries, and gain control over your financial journey. EPT is designed to make expense tracking seamless and hassle-free. No need to provide unnecessary personal information – just your email and name are all we require to help you take charge of your expenses.


Join EPT today and embark on a path towards financial empowerment. Sign up or log in now to experience the convenience of managing your finances with ease!
